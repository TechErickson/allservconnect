Our Story

From a Nairobi startup to a regional force

AllServ Tech was born out of frustration. Our founders watched too many great businesses fail online — not because of bad products, but because they couldn't access quality tech talent and honest digital strategy. So they built the agency they wished existed.

Starting with just three people and a shared workspace in Westlands, we've grown into a team of 20+ specialists spanning web engineering, AI development, design, and SEO. Every hire has been deliberate — people who are excellent at what they do and genuinely care about client outcomes.

Today we serve clients ranging from early-stage startups to established enterprises across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and internationally. Our reputation is built on two things: results and relationships.

About Us/Our Values
Our Values

What we stand for — every single day.

Values aren't a poster on the wall. They're the decisions we make when no one is watching and the standards we hold ourselves to on every project.

These six principles shape how we work, how we communicate, and how we measure success. They're not aspirational — they're operational.

01

Client First

Every decision starts with one question: what's best for the client?

We don't upsell for the sake of revenue. We recommend what genuinely serves your goals, even if that means a simpler solution.

02

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standard in code, design, and communication.

Good enough is never good enough. We review, refine, and polish until we're proud to put our name on it.

03

Innovation

We stay ahead of the curve so you don't have to.

From AI tooling to new frameworks, we actively invest in learning and pass that advantage directly to our clients.

04

Transparency

No hidden costs, no jargon, no surprises.

You'll always know exactly where your project stands — timelines, budgets, blockers. Radical honesty builds lasting trust.

05

Impact

We measure our work by real outcomes — traffic, leads, and growth you can see.

We tie our success to yours. If your numbers aren't moving in the right direction, we haven't done our job.

06

Integrity

We do what we say, say what we mean, and treat every client with respect.

Our word is our bond. We'd rather lose a deal than make a promise we can't keep.
